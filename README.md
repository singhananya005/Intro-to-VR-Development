# Intro-to-VR-Development
My name is Ananya singh, and I am a first year Btech student with a growing interest in technology and programming. I have recently started learning about Github.
I am interested in areas like web development,virtual reality,other platforms of coding.As a beginner, I am continuously improving my skills in programming languages.

I am eager to learn,collaborate,and build innovative projects in the future.

I am grateful to IEEE and IEEE WIE for organizing such an insightful and forward looking sessions of VR.


Working in a VR workshop was an exciting and deeply informative experience that allowed me to explore the world of virtual reality in a practical way. The workshop, organized by IEEE and Women in Engineering, was well-structured and focused on building both conceptual understanding and hands-on skills. It started with the basic introduction to VR, where I learned how virtual environments are created and how users can interact with them using different devices and technologies. Gradually, the sessions moved towards practical implementation, which made the learning process even more interesting.
One of the most valuable parts of the workshop was working with tools like Unity and Blender. In Unity, I learned how to build simple VR environments, add objects, and understand how scenes function. It gave me a basic idea of game development and interactive design. On the other hand, Blender helped me understand 3D modeling, where I created basic shapes, modified them, and learned how to navigate in a 3D workspace. Initially, I found it difficult to handle the controls and remember shortcuts, especially while working on my first beginner face model, but with practice, I started getting more comfortable.
The workshop also improved my problem-solving ability and patience, as working in VR and 3D design requires attention to detail and continuous practice. It encouraged me to think creatively and visualize ideas in a new way. I also learned the importance of teamwork and communication while discussing tasks and sharing ideas with other participants.
Overall, the VR workshop was a very enriching experience for me. It not only enhanced my technical knowledge but also increased my interest in emerging technologies like virtual reality. I am thankful to the organizers for providing such a great learning platform, and this experience has motivated me to explore more in this field in the future.
